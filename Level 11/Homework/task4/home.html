Baseball

Baseball is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of nine players each. The game is played on a field with four bases arranged in a diamond shape, and the objective is to score runs by hitting the ball and running around the bases. Karate

Karate is a striking-based martial art that originated in Okinawa, Japan, and focuses on punches, kicks, knee strikes, and open-hand techniques. Governed by the World Karate Federation (WKF), it is practiced worldwide and was featured in the Olympics for the first time in 2021. Boxing

Boxing is a combat sport in which two fighters compete using only their fists to strike their opponent while wearing padded gloves. The sport has ancient origins but developed into its modern form in the 19th century with the introduction of the Marquess of Queensberry rules. Boxing matches are divided into rounds, and fighters aim to win by knockout (KO), technical knockout (TKO), or judges' decision. Governed by organizations like the WBA, WBC, IBF, and WBO, boxing features legendary fighters such as Muhammad Ali, Mike Tyson, and Floyd Mayweather.
